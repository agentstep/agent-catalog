# Commercial Legal

Reviews vendor agreements, NDAs, and SaaS subscriptions against your sales-side or purchasing-side playbook, tracks renewals and cancel-by deadlines before they're missed, routes escalations to the right approver, and translates reviews into summaries business stakeholders will actually read.

## Skills

- **amendment-history** -- Trace how a contract has changed across its base agreement and all amendments
- **cold-start-interview** -- Learn your commercial contracts practice and write the team practice profile
- **customize** -- Change one setting in the practice profile without re-running the full cold-start interview
- **escalation-flagger** -- Route a contract issue to the right approver per the escalation matrix and draft the ask
- **matter-workspace** -- Manage matter workspaces to keep one client or engagement's context separate from every other
- **nda-review** -- Fast triage of inbound NDAs into GREEN / YELLOW / RED so the team only spends lawyer time where needed
- **renewal-tracker** -- Show contracts with cancel-by deadlines coming up and warn before notice windows close
- **review-proposals** -- Review and approve or reject pending playbook update proposals from the playbook-monitor agent
- **review** -- Review a vendor agreement, NDA, or SaaS subscription against your playbook
- **saas-msa-review** -- Review SaaS subscription agreements with attention to auto-renewal, price escalation, and data terms
- **stakeholder-summary** -- Translate a contract review into a summary the business stakeholder will actually read
- **vendor-agreement-review** -- Review an inbound vendor agreement against the team playbook, flag deviations, and assess risk

## Agents

- **deal-debrief** -- Weekly agent that surfaces recently signed agreements containing playbook deviations and prompts the attorney to log context while memory is fresh
- **playbook-monitor** -- Data-triggered agent that watches the deviation log and proposes playbook updates when a clause position has been deviated from enough times
- **renewal-watcher** -- Scheduled agent that checks the renewal register weekly and posts what's coming up before cancel-by windows close
