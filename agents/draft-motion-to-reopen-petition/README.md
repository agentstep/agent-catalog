# Draft Motion to Reopen Petition — managed-agent template

Drafts a Motion to Reopen removal proceedings for filing in Immigration Court under INA Section 240(c)(7) and 8 CFR 1003.23. Addresses the legal standards for reopening, including changed country conditions, ineffective assistance of counsel, and exceptional circumstances for in absentia orders.

## What it does

1. Reads the prior removal order, hearing transcript, and case history
2. Analyzes the legal basis for reopening and applicable exceptions
3. Evaluates timeliness under the 90-day/180-day deadlines and exceptions
4. Drafts the motion with supporting legal arguments and evidence references
5. Prepares the declaration/affidavit and proposed evidence list
6. Produces the motion document and a filing checklist

## Inputs

- Prior removal order or decision from Immigration Court
- Case history (A-number, hearing dates, prior filings, representation history)
- Basis for reopening (new evidence, changed country conditions, ineffective assistance, in absentia rescission)
- Supporting evidence or declarations

## Outputs

- `./out/motion-to-reopen.md` — the motion document with legal arguments
- `./out/mtr-filing-checklist.md` — filing requirements and deadline calculations
