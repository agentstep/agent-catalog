# AI Governance Legal

Triages proposed AI use cases against your registry, runs impact assessments across the regimes in scope, reviews vendor AI terms for training-on-data and liability gaps, and keeps your AI policy current with practice.

## Skills

- **ai-inventory** -- EU AI Act per-system inventory; track each AI system's role and risk tier (prohibited, high-risk, limited, minimal, GPAI, GPAI+systemic)
- **aia-generation** -- Run an AI impact assessment with structured intake, risk analysis, regulatory classification per regime in scope, and recommendation
- **cold-start-interview** -- Learn your AI governance practice and write the practice profile from your existing policies and risk posture
- **customize** -- Change one setting in the practice profile without re-running the full cold-start interview
- **matter-workspace** -- Manage matter workspaces to keep one client or engagement's context separate from every other
- **policy-monitor** -- Weekly sweep of saved AIAs, triage results, and vendor reviews to find policy drift against current practice
- **policy-starter** -- Draft a firm AI usage policy from published model policies, adapted to your practice profile
- **reg-gap-analysis** -- Diff a new AI regulation or guidance against your current governance posture and surface gaps with remediation plans
- **use-case-triage** -- Classify a proposed AI use case against your registry as approved, conditional, or not approved
- **vendor-ai-review** -- Review vendor AI terms for training-on-data, liability, model changes, and AI policy gaps
