# Product Legal

Reviews product launches against your risk calibration, answers "is this a problem?" questions in minutes, checks marketing copy for claims that need substantiation, and flags upcoming launches that need legal eyes before anyone asks.

## Skills

- **cold-start-interview** -- Connect to your launch tracker, read past reviews, and learn your risk calibration
- **customize** -- Change one setting in the practice profile without re-running the full cold-start interview
- **feature-risk-assessment** -- Deeper risk assessment for a single feature or product area when the launch review found something that needs more analysis
- **is-this-a-problem** -- Fast answer for the quick Slack question; pattern-matches against your calibration
- **launch-review** -- Full launch review against your framework and risk calibration
- **marketing-claims-review** -- Review marketing copy for claims that need substantiation, reframing, or cutting
- **matter-workspace** -- Manage matter workspaces to keep one client or engagement's context separate

## Agents

- **launch-watcher** -- Daily agent that monitors the launch tracker for upcoming launches that likely need legal review and flags them before product counsel gets surprised
