# Corporate Legal

Runs M&A diligence at scale with cited tabular review, builds disclosure schedules and closing checklists, drafts board consents and minutes in house format, and tracks entity compliance deadlines across jurisdictions.

## Skills

- **ai-tool-handoff** -- Detect when Luminance, Kira, or a similar bulk-review tool is in use; hand off high-volume extraction and QA its output
- **board-minutes** -- Draft board or committee meeting minutes in house format with auto-detection of upcoming meetings
- **closing-checklist** -- Maintain the closing checklist with status, critical path, and days to close; self-updating from diligence findings
- **cold-start-interview** -- House cold-start interview for deal-specific or practice-level context setup
- **customize** -- Change one setting in the practice profile without re-running the full cold-start interview
- **deal-team-summary** -- Aggregate diligence findings into a deal team briefing at the right altitude for the audience
- **diligence-issue-extraction** -- Read VDR documents and extract issues per house categories and materiality thresholds
- **entity-compliance** -- Entity compliance tracker with upcoming deadlines, status updates, health audits, and CSV export
- **integration-management** -- Post-closing M&A integration tracker with phased workplans, consent tracking, and contract assignment
- **material-contract-schedule** -- Build the material contracts disclosure schedule from diligence findings
- **matter-workspace** -- Manage matter workspaces to keep one deal's context separate from every other
- **tabular-review** -- One row per document, one column per data point, every cell cited to source; built for M&A diligence at scale
- **written-consent** -- Draft unanimous written consent of the board or a committee in house format

## Agents

- **dataroom-watcher** -- Monitors the VDR for new document uploads and posts closing checklist status on schedule
