# Privacy Legal

Triages processing activities, generates PIAs, reviews DPAs as controller or processor, drafts DSAR responses within statutory timelines, and monitors policy drift against practice.

## Skills

- **cold-start-interview** -- Learn your privacy practice and write the practice profile from your policy, DPA template, and a reference PIA
- **customize** -- Change one setting in the practice profile without re-running the full cold-start interview
- **dpa-review** -- Review a Data Processing Agreement against your DPA playbook; auto-detects controller or processor role
- **dsar-response** -- Walk through a Data Subject Access Request (deletion, portability, correction) and draft the response within statutory timelines
- **matter-workspace** -- Manage matter workspaces to keep one client or engagement's context separate
- **pia-generation** -- Generate a Privacy Impact Assessment in house format for a new feature, product, or processing activity
- **policy-monitor** -- Keep the privacy policy current with practice via weekly sweeps or direct queries for proposed changes
- **reg-gap-analysis** -- Diff a new or changed regulation against current privacy policy and practice; output gap list with remediation plan
- **use-case-triage** -- Determine whether a processing activity needs a PIA, a mandatory GDPR DPIA, or can proceed
