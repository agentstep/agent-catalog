# Competitive Intelligence — managed-agent template

Monitors competitor websites, changelogs, and public communications to produce structured comparison matrices and strategic insights.

## What it does

1. Tracks competitor product updates from changelogs, blogs, and press releases
2. Extracts feature announcements and pricing changes
3. Builds feature comparison matrices across the competitive landscape
4. Identifies positioning shifts and messaging changes
5. Produces weekly intelligence briefs highlighting key competitive movements
6. Flags urgent items (competitor launching in your market segment, pricing undercut)
