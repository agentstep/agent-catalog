# Privacy Policy Auditor — managed-agent template

Compares privacy policies against GDPR and CCPA requirements, identifies missing disclosures, and recommends specific language additions for compliance.

## What it does

1. Reads the company's current privacy policy document
2. Maps each GDPR article and CCPA section to required disclosures
3. Checks the policy for presence and adequacy of each required element
4. Identifies missing disclosures and vague language that may not satisfy regulators
5. Recommends specific additions or rewording with draft language
6. Produces a compliance scorecard with gap severity ratings
