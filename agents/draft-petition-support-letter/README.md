# Draft Petition Support Letter — managed-agent template

Drafts a comprehensive expert opinion letter in support of an EB-1A Extraordinary Ability petition under INA Section 203(b)(1)(A). The letter is structured to address the Kazarian two-step analysis framework and demonstrate that the beneficiary meets at least 3 of the 10 regulatory criteria at 8 CFR 204.5(h)(3).

## What it does

1. Reads the beneficiary's CV, publications, awards, and other evidence
2. Identifies which of the 10 EB-1A criteria can be substantiated
3. Maps evidence to each claimed criterion with specificity
4. Drafts an expert opinion letter following the Kazarian two-step framework
5. Addresses both the initial evidence review and the final merits determination
6. Produces the letter and an evidence mapping document

## Inputs

- Beneficiary CV/resume with complete publication and award history
- Expert's credentials and relationship to the field (for the letter's author identity)
- Evidence package (publications, citations, media coverage, awards, membership documentation)
- Target EB-1A criteria being claimed

## Outputs

- `./out/eb1a-support-letter.md` — the expert opinion letter
- `./out/eb1a-evidence-map.md` — criterion-to-evidence mapping with strength assessment
