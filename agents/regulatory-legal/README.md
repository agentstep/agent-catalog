# Regulatory Legal

Watches regulatory feeds, diffs new rules against your policy library, tracks comment deadlines and open gaps, and writes the digest your team reads Monday morning.

## Skills

- **cold-start-interview** -- Build your watchlist, index the policy library, and learn your materiality threshold so the monitor surfaces signal instead of noise
- **comments** -- Review open NPRM comment periods, log decisions, and track deadlines
- **customize** -- Change one setting in the practice profile without re-running the full cold-start interview
- **gap-surfacer** -- Shared gap- and comment-tracker framework; tracks open policy gaps with remediation status
- **gaps** -- Open gaps tracker showing what is flagged and not yet closed with remediation status
- **matter-workspace** -- Manage matter workspaces to keep one engagement's context separate from another
- **policy-diff** -- Diff a specific regulatory change against the indexed policy library to find which policies it touches
- **policy-redraft** -- Produce a proposed marked-up policy redraft that closes a gap found by gap analysis
- **reg-feed-watcher** -- Check regulatory feeds now and report what is new since the last check, filtered by materiality threshold

## Agents

- **reg-change-monitor** -- Scheduled agent that checks regulatory feeds and posts a filtered digest at the configured cadence
