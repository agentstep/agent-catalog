# Litigation Legal

Manages the litigation portfolio -- matters, deadlines, holds, demands, outside counsel -- and does the work: claim charts (patent and civil), chronologies, depo prep, privilege logs, brief drafting. Adapts to how you work litigation: in-house, firm, or solo.

## Skills

- **brief-section-drafter** -- Draft a brief section in house style, consistent with the case theory, with every fact cited and every case checked
- **chronology** -- Build or update a chronology from document sources; dated events extracted, de-duped, and tagged by significance
- **claim-chart** -- Build or review patent claim charts or civil element charts with pin cites and gap detection
- **cold-start-interview** -- House cold-start for the litigation plugin; branches by role and side, captures risk calibration and house style
- **customize** -- Change one setting in the practice profile without re-running the full cold-start interview
- **demand-draft** -- Draft a demand letter from a completed intake with privilege/FRE 408/waiver gate and .docx output
- **demand-intake** -- Pre-drafting context gathering for a demand letter covering parties, facts, basis, leverage, and BATNA
- **demand-received** -- Triage an inbound demand letter; extract fields, assess merit, and present response options
- **deposition-prep** -- Build a deposition outline for a witness with topics organized around the case theory
- **legal-hold** -- Issue, refresh, release, or report on legal holds with .docx notices
- **matter-briefing** -- Deep briefing on one matter with current posture, changes, next deadline, and risk re-assessment
- **matter-close** -- Close a matter with outcome capture, final exposure, and lessons learned
- **matter-intake** -- Intake a new matter with conflicts, risk triage, materiality, and legal hold assessment
- **matter-update** -- Append a dated event to a matter's history and refresh the log row
- **matter-workspace** -- Manage matter workspaces for multi-client practices
- **oc-status** -- Generate weekly status-request email drafts to outside counsel across the portfolio
- **portfolio-status** -- Roll up the portfolio with risk distribution, upcoming deadlines, and stale matter detection
- **privilege-log-review** -- First-pass privilege log review; make obvious calls and flag hard ones for attorney review
- **subpoena-triage** -- Triage a subpoena with scope/burden/privilege analysis and objections framework

## Agents

- **docket-watcher** -- Scheduled agent that watches court dockets, flags new filings, computes candidate deadlines, and cross-references against matter history
