# Employment Legal

Reviews hires and terminations for jurisdiction-specific risk flags, classifies workers against the controlling state test, tracks leave deadlines before they're missed, runs internal investigations, and drafts policies with state supplements where the law differs.

## Skills

- **cold-start-interview** -- Learn your jurisdictional footprint and escalation rules from your handbook and termination memos
- **customize** -- Change one setting in the practice profile without re-running the full cold-start interview
- **expansion-kickoff** -- Kick off international expansion planning for a new country with EOR vs. entity framing
- **expansion-update** -- Update status of an in-progress international expansion; recalculate what is unblocked
- **handbook-updates** -- Diff a proposed handbook change against the current version; flag ripple effects and state supplement impacts
- **hiring-review** -- Review an offer letter and restrictive covenants with jurisdiction-specific checks
- **internal-investigation** -- Shared framework for managing internal investigations from intake through final memo
- **international-expansion** -- Implementation-planning framework for international hiring with EOR vs. entity decision framing
- **investigation-add** -- Add documents, interview notes, or observations to an open investigation
- **investigation-memo** -- Draft or update the privileged investigation memo from the investigation log
- **investigation-open** -- Open a new internal investigation matter with intake and sources checklist
- **investigation-query** -- Ask questions against an open investigation log about witness statements, conflicts, and gaps
- **investigation-summary** -- Draft an audience-specific summary (HR, leadership, or outside counsel) from the investigation memo
- **leave-tracker** -- Check open leaves for deadline alerts and required decisions
- **log-leave** -- Add a new leave to the register with minimum information needed to start tracking deadlines
- **matter-workspace** -- Manage matter workspaces to keep one client or engagement's context separate
- **policy-drafting** -- Draft an employment policy with state supplements where law differs across the jurisdictional footprint
- **termination-review** -- High-risk flag detection, severance and release review, and final pay timing by jurisdiction
- **wage-hour-qa** -- Jurisdiction-aware wage/hour Q&A covering classification, overtime, meal/rest breaks, and final pay
- **worker-classification** -- Classify a proposed worker engagement by running the applicable jurisdiction tests

## Agents

- **leave-tracker** -- Weekly agent that monitors open employee leaves with hard legal deadlines and fires decision-point alerts before deadlines are missed
